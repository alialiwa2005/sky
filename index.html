The Story of Sky.

Sky's Roadmap.

19 May 2020

Sky Tea is Founded.

We are a start-up tea business, founded by a 15 year old & his parakeet, Sky Blue, in May of 2020. 

20 May 2020

The Start of Sky's Operation.

Sky's logo is designed, and sky's slogan, "Hand-Crafted Fair-Trade Organic Teas;" is formed.

The domain name, SkyTea.ml, is purchased; and Sky's primary business email, hello@SkyTea.ml, is set-up.
Sky's official merchandise store is created & launched on Bonfire.

Sky begins partnerships with global tea suppliers.

23 March 2020

Sky Flys into the Internet.

Sky's website is designed & launched, running on the domain name, SkyTea.ml.

Development begins on Sky's Virtual Tea Shop. Anticipated completion of online tea store is in early August of 2020.

Genuine

Our teas are grown natively throughout and are imported from around the Earth.

Fair-Trade

All tea sold by us is certified Fair-Trade.
Your purchases of our teas and tea blends support local farmers & their loved ones, throughout the globe.

Organic

Sky's teas and tea-blends are grown and harvested in accordance with the USDA National Organic Program; certified Organic by Quality Assurance International (QAI).

...all while supplying yourself with the full, pure & unmodified proper delights of tea.
